About ISSH

Why ISSH?

The International School of Schaffhausen provides a caring, personalised learning environment where every student is seen and supported. With small classes and dedicated teachers, we deliver a world-class IB education in the heart of Northern Switzerland.

  • English-language education within the IB framework
  • Personalised learning with small class sizes
  • International families alongside local Swiss families
  • A safe, caring environment focused on whole-child growth

School Life & Academics

IB Programmes

The IB framework runs across all divisions, with a focus on inquiry, critical thinking, intercultural understanding, and student agency.

Early Learning Centre

Nurturing curiosity and confidence through play-based, inquiry-led learning for our youngest learners ages 3-5.

Primary Years Programme (PYP)

Inquiry-led learning that builds curiosity, confidence, and social-emotional development for ages 5-12.

Secondary & Pre-IB

A bespoke secondary pathway grounded in IB philosophy, with Pre-IB in grades 9-10 and a strong STEM focus.

Diploma Programme (DP)

A rigorous two-year programme for ages 16-19, preparing students for university and beyond.

Outstanding IB Results

ISSH students consistently achieve excellent IB Diploma results, with a 100% pass rate for several consecutive years and outcomes well above the world average.

100% Pass rate

Boarding Programme

Neighbourhood-style boarding

ISSH boarding goes beyond accommodation. It is a safe, nurturing environment designed to build independence, responsibility, and life skills in a neighbourhood setting off campus.

  • Independence and real-world responsibility
  • Small groups of around 10 students
  • Daily cultural exchange in a global community
  • Woven into the educational concept
